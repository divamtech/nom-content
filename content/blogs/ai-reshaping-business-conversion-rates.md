## The Reality Check: Common Pitfalls to Avoid

❌ **Mistake 1:** Deploying AI without clean data. Garbage in = garbage out.
✅ **Solution:** Invest in data quality and integration first.

❌ **Mistake 2:** Implementing AI without clear business metrics.
✅ **Solution:** Define conversion metrics, baseline performance, and success criteria upfront.

❌ **Mistake 3:** Over-personalizing without privacy/consent framework.
✅ **Solution:** Transparent data practices build trust and compliance.

❌ **Mistake 4:** Deploying AI and expecting immediate ROI.
✅ **Solution:** Allow 3-6 months for data accumulation and model training.

❌ **Mistake 5:** Underinvesting in change management and team training.
✅ **Solution:** Your team is as important as the technology.
