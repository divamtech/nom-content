AI agents are no longer a category waiting for proof. The proof is in the production systems already absorbing the work no human team enjoyed: the document review, the memo drafting, the inbox triage, the lead scoring, the protocol summarisation. What follows is a structured tour of twenty-five agents we have engineered, deployed and indexed in our retrieval layer, grouped by the function they serve.

## Why Agents, Why Now

* Businesses need leverage, not headcount  
* Repetitive work is silently consuming the time of your most expensive people  
* Smart workflow automation is becoming the default competitive moat  
* The cost curve has crossed: agents are now cheaper than the alternatives they replace

These are not chatbots. They are autonomous systems trained to complete workflows, fetch data, interpret context and act with the discipline of a domain expert.

## Finance and Investment

* **Investment Memo Generator** - converts data rooms and primary research into board-ready memos  
* **Buy-Side / Sell-Side Agent** - comparable transaction analysis with citations  
* **Due Diligence Assistant** - checklist-driven diligence with red-flag surfacing  
* **10-Q / 10-K Document Extractor** - structured extraction at filing speed  
* **Competitive Analysis Assistant** - continuous monitoring with strategic deltas

## Operations and HR

* **Spreadsheet AI Assistant** - natural-language operations on production workbooks  
* **AI Staffing Assistant** - open-role triage and shortlist generation  
* **Staff Training Assistant** - tailored learning paths grounded in internal documentation  
* **Infosec Compliance Agent** - control mapping, evidence gathering and gap reporting  
* **Slackbot Knowledge Agent** - answers grounded in your wiki, with cited sources

## Sales and Marketing

* **Customer Support Chatbot** - intent-aware, sentiment-aware, escalation-ready  
* **RFP Response Assistant** - first-draft responses anchored in past wins and approved language  
* **Tender Document Analyser** - clause-by-clause review with risk flags  
* **PostgreSQL Query Assistant** - natural-language to validated SQL with safety rails  
* **Lead Scoring Agent** - predictive scoring tuned to your ICP and historical conversion data

## Healthcare and Legal

* **Patient Reports Assistant** - clinician-style summaries with audit trail  
* **Call Centre QA Agent** - 100% call coverage with cited evidence per finding  
* **SOAP Notes Generator** - structured clinical notes generated in the room  
* **Protocol Summariser** - fast, consistent reads of long study documents

## Growth and Automation

* **AI Writing Assistant** - brand-voice enforcement at the document level  
* **Programmatic SEO Tool** - thousands of high-quality pages from a single keyword sheet  
* **Resume-to-Job Match Agent** - semantic matching against a live role pool  
* **Invoice Data Extractor** - OCR plus LLM parsing with structured JSON output  
* **Meeting Summariser Agent** - minutes, decisions and follow-ups in five minutes

## What These Twenty-Five Have in Common

* A bounded scope and a clear definition of done  
* Retrieval grounding so outputs cite source material  
* Human-in-the-loop above defined risk thresholds  
* Cost controls and per-session token quotas  
* Audit logs that survive a regulatory or internal review
