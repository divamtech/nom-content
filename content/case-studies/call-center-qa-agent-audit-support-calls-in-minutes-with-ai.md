# Call Center QA Agent – Audit Support Calls in Minutes with AI

*by Divam Digest*

---

Listening to support calls for quality assurance and compliance is one of the most time-consuming tasks for QA teams. At DivamTech, we built a smart Call Center QA Agent that automates call auditing with speed and precision — without losing context or compliance accuracy.

## 📞 The Problem

- Manual QA reviews consume hundreds of hours every month
- Human reviewers can miss compliance violations or quality issues
- Auditing every customer interaction is not scalable
- Delayed feedback impacts agent performance and customer satisfaction

## 🤖 The AI Agent Solution

Our Call Center QA Agent automates the entire review workflow by:

- Accepting uploaded support call recordings
- Transcribing conversations into searchable text
- Evaluating calls against QA and compliance policies
- Detecting flagged moments and risky interactions
- Generating detailed QA/compliance reports instantly

## ⚙️ How It Works

- **Interface:** Form-based audio upload system
- **LLM Used:** AWS Bedrock – Claude 3.5 Sonnet
- **Data Source:** Uploaded customer support call recordings
- **Processing:** Speech-to-text + AI-driven QA analysis
- **Output:** Compliance scorecard and flagged interaction report
- **Time to Launch:** Easy

## 📈 Business Impact

- ⏱ Reduced QA analysis workload from 100 hours/month to just 4
- ⚖️ Improved compliance monitoring and policy adherence
- 📊 Enabled scalable auditing across all customer calls
- 👨‍🏫 Freed QA teams to focus on coaching and strategy instead of repetitive reviews
