*by Divam Digest*

---

Manual invoice processing is **time-consuming, error-prone, and expensive**.

At **DivamTech**, we built the **Invoice Data Extractor Agent** to eliminate bottlenecks in AP/AR workflows and bring structure to unstructured data.

---

## 🧾 The Problem

- Invoices arrive in multiple formats: **PDF scans, emails, screenshots**  
- Finance teams manually extract:
  - Dates  
  - Totals  
  - Taxes  
  - Vendor details  
- Manual entry leads to **delays and errors**  

---

## 🤖 The AI Agent Solution

Our Invoice Extractor AI agent:

- Accepts invoice uploads (**PDF, PNG, etc.**)  
- Uses **OCR + LLM parsing** to extract key fields  
- Outputs **structured JSON data** ready for integration  

---

## ⚙️ System Blueprint

- **Interface:** Upload form  
- **LLMs Used:** OpenAI GPT-4o + OCR layer  
- **Output Format:** JSON  
  - Vendor Name  
  - Date  
  - GST  
  - Total Amount  
  - Line Items  
- **Time to Launch:** Easy
